Data Science Intern

Overview

Gnip is a fast growing start-up at the center of the Social Media and Big Data revolution. Gnip's software collects, processes and delivers hundreds of millions of social media activities a day. We are looking for a Data Science Intern to help collect, prepare, analyze and visualize gobs of Social Media data. Your work will help Gnip's customers understand the value and opportunity of using social media data to track brand sentiment, understand the spread of social memes, create signals for automated stock trading systems, monitor natural disasters as well as hundreds of other applications.
