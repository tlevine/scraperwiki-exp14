Job Title:  Campaign Analyst, New York

We are seeking a results-oriented, entrepreneurial and highly driven Campaign Analyst (CA) with 2+ years experience in digital media, specifically in campaign data analysis and management. The role will be based in New York, but will work alongside our global team of analysts. The aim of the team is to deliver our clients world-class analysis, advice and overall success management to ensure they achieve results using our software.
 

The analyst will provide detailed analysis of client campaigns, delivering optimization advice and best-practice training, whilst also sometimes managing campaigns on behalf of clients in the initial on-boarding period. It is imperative that our largest clients deeply understand the Facebook advertising platform as well as our software, and as a client-facing role this position will play a pivotal part in managing these relationships to achieve both of these objectives.
 

The right candidate will be very comfortable with the metrics used in digital media and will be proficient in managing and optimizing digital advertising campaigns to achieve a broad range of marketing objectives. A proven understanding of where Facebook sits in this marketing mix, or ideally hands-on experience with the platform, is a big plus.
 

The Campaign Analyst role is part of the BuyBuddy team providing advertisers with software to create, monitor and optimize highly-targeted Facebook advertising campaigns.  The Campaign Analyst will provide detailed analysis for some of the biggest advertisers on the Facebook platform, from advertising agencies such as Group M, PHD and OMD, to the largest social games companies.

 

Responsibilities:
• Work within a driven and highly motivated team to provide a world-class client experience for some of the world's largest agencies and brands.
• Research and maintain expertise in the social media marketing landscape and understand how we fit into the wider marketing mix.
• Analyse client data to find trends and provide campaign optimisation advice.
• Effectively demonstrate and train clients on BuyBuddy’s functionality and features, empowering them to become self-sufficient on the platform.
• Listen to, identify and understand customer objectives and tailor communication to meet stated and unstated needs and expectations.
• Assist clients in developing Facebook advertising strategy and help them to employ best practices.
• Maintain effective, revenue-generating client relationships with those clients that have been offered our premium service.
• Manage and optimize large-scale Facebook performance campaigns as needed.


Qualifications:
• Minimum 3+ years experience in digital media
• Experience working at an ad agency is preferable
• Strong professional ethics and integrity
• Experience in managing large online display and/or direct advertising campaigns
• Strong quantitative and analytical skills
• Solid and proven understanding of digital marketing and advertising, with a particularly strong grasp of where social marketing and technology fits in these larger strategies
• Comfortable and proven understanding of the technologies already in the market
• Excellent interpersonal, oral and written communication skills
• Ability to work in a fast-paced, results-oriented, team environment
• Presentation acumen and the ability to clearly communicate concepts
• Excellent organisational and time management skills
• Excellent computer skills, especially with Excel
• Willing to travel
• Bachelor’s Degree
• Experience with CRM systems, such as Salesforce.com, a plus
• Experience in managing Facebook advertising campaigns, a big plus


Buddy Media Inc. offers competitive compensation and a comprehensive benefits package including: health insurance, retirement plans, paid time off and participation in the company equity plan. All benefits listed are subject to change. Buddy Media Inc. is an equal opportunity employer.
