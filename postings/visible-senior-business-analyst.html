Senior Business Analyst

Conducts and interprets quantitative and qualitative analysis of traditional and consumer generated media using computer skills including: Excel pivot table manipulation, attribute lookups, data sampling, competitive indexing. Writes Boolean-based searches and query strings to set parameters on the data universe relevant to business questions. Creates reports profiling consumer preferences, brand acceptance and reputation. Reviews existing marketing processes of clients and analysis of data to create marketing strategies and alternative business solution for clients. Requires Master’s degree in Global Marketing Communications & Advertising or related and 1 yr. exp. in job duties. Visible Technologies, 154 Middlesex Turnpikes, MA 01803.
