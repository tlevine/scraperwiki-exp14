Careers

We're building a killer team and a world-class product from our headquarters in New York City, and we're looking for great people to join us. Please email us at jobs@offerpop.com if you're interested in working at Offerpop.

Sales Representative

As a sales professional at Offerpop, you'll work directly with marketers, brand managers, and agencies to help them understand and achieve the benefits of social media marketing.

Responsibilities:

  • Prospect, develop opportunities, demonstrate the product, and close sales.
  • Target at least 4 hours per day on the phone with customers and prospects.
  • Consistent, diligent follow-up with customers throughout the sales process.
  • Build a deep understanding of the product, the market, and the competition.
  • Work with customers to understand their needs, establish your credibility as a trusted advisor, and help them achieve their goals with Offerpop.
  • Manage customer opportunities and quota/revenue responsibilities from end-to-end in a defined territory.

Qualifications:

  • Self-starter, with incredible drive and work ethic.
  • Friendly and engaging, relationships come naturally.
  • Smart, with ability to ask probing questions, learn quickly, and solve problems.
  • Organized and focused; you can identify and develop accounts from scratch, manage a pipeline, and consistently close business on your own.
  • A passion for web technology and social media, and the capacity to understand how it works.
  • You're a person of the highest integrity.
  • 0-4 years of experience.
  • B.S. or B.A. degree.

Compensation:

  • Base salary + uncapped commissions.
  • Stock options package.
  • Health & dental insurance, 401(k) plan, and commuter benefits.

To apply, send your resume to jobs@offerpop.com.

Director of Business Development

The Director of Business Development (a new position at Offerpop) will have overall responsibility for partner strategy, partner recruitment/sales and management. The role requires a strategic mindset, a sales orientation, and strong understanding of the social and digital marketing landscape. Success in this role is based on driving revenue through partner channels, and driving partner influenced revenue.

Responsibilities:

  • Define, develop and execute the strategy for partnerships and alliances, in alignment with the company’s overall strategy.
  • Build, prioritize and manage a pipeline of partnership opportunities. This includes relationships with OEM and distribution partners, technology partners, agencies and affiliates in the US and internationally.
  • Provide regular updates to the leadership team on the progress of partnership opportunities, and the expected impact on customer acquisition and revenue targets.
  • Close partnership deals, and lead cross-functional teams to manage the onboarding, enablement and joint go-to-market activity with partners.
  • Track, analyze and communicate key metrics, including partner-driven revenue and partner-influenced revenue.

Qualifications:

  • 5+ years of experience building and managing revenue generating partnerships in the SaaS or software industry. The ideal candidate’s background will also include B2B technology sales experience, preferably in digital marketing, ad tech or SaaS technology.
  • Self-starter and ability to lead initiatives on your own -- as an independent contributor, and as a cross-functional team leader. You take a hands-on approach; you’re a doer, not a delegator.
  • Proven experience modeling partnership opportunities, and negotiating complex deals.
  • Ability to develop and nurture strong relationships with executives and sales teams.
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills.
  • Highly organized, with strong attention to detail and flawless follow through.
  • Bachelor’s degree required. MBA a plus.
  • Must be willing to do some travel.
  • Excellent partner/customer and employer references.

Compensation:

  • Base salary + uncapped commissions.
  • Stock options package.
  • Health & dental insurance, 401(k) plan, and commuter benefits.

To apply, send your resume to jobs@offerpop.com.

Business Analytics Manager

Offerpop is seeking a hands-on Business Analytics Manager due to explosive growth in 2011. As the Business Analytics Manager, you will be an integral part of the Marketing team at Offerpop working with senior leaders across Sales, Finance, and Development. The ideal candidate will be a self-starter and process-oriented with a high level of enthusiasm and professionalism.

Responsibilities:

  • Provide cross-functional teams appropriate data to make sound business decisions and refine efficient processes. This includes building key metrics, analyzing trends and providing timely reporting to managers.
  • Manage reporting and dashboard needs by providing real-time metrics on site traffic, lead conversion, sales forecasting, purchasing behaviors, and waterfall metrics.
  • Responsible for analyzing and reporting on KPIs such as churn, CAC and LTV, and help to drive customer segmentation and cohort analysis
  • Serve as one of the Salesforce system administrators, as it relates to supporting Sales and Marketing.

Qualifications:

  • 3-5 years of relevant work experience.
  • Previous experience in a high-tech software company, SaaS and SEO experience is a plus.
  • Bachelor’s Degree and expertise in the Financial Planning & Analysis role.
  • Direct experience working as a business partner to Sales and Marketing organizations is a plus.
  • Must possess excellent written and verbal communication skills, project management capabilities, and ability to manage cross-functional issues.
  • Must have a sense of urgency and excellent time management skills.
  • Self-starter and ability to lead without the need for direct authority.
  • Strong attention to detail and flawless follow through.
  • Ability to plan and organize work processes, manage priorities and meet commitments.
  • High level of computer skills including Google Analytics, Salesforce, Google Spreadsheets, Excel, Word, Outlook and PowerPoint. Experience with SaaS-based BI or analytics tools, such as GoodData or RJMetrics, is a plus.

To apply, send your resume to jobs@offerpop.com.

Social Media Consultant

As a Social Media Consultant, you'll work with our sales and business development teams to help win new business, and you'll work with our top customers to make them successful. This requires an ability to listen intently and understand customer objectives, propose solutions based on Offerpop's technology, and help customers create and execute successful social media marketing campaigns. As a Consultant, you will play key roles in the pre-sales process and ongoing management of clients, while working with the product team to optimize the business value derived from our platform.

Responsibilities:

  • Work hand-in-hand with the sales and business development teams on key opportunities.
  • Participate in phone calls and web demos with customers and agencies, probe to understand needs/objectives, and position the Offerpop solution.
  • Generate creative concepts, and help customers create and execute campaigns. This includes some "hands on" HTML and CSS customizations, and lightweight image editing.
  • Help customers interpret the results and learn from their campaigns.
  • Share customer feedback with our product team, and work with them to inform/shape the product.
  • Assist long-term commitment clients to maximize the value of engaging with Offerpop.

Qualifications:

  • 4+ years of digital marketing experience, including 2+ years in social media marketing. Agency experience a plus.
  • Deep understanding of the Facebook platform for marketing.
  • Experience working with HTML, CSS and Photoshop. JavaScript experience a plus.
  • Strong verbal and written communications skills, and the presence to interact with senior executives.
  • Bachelor's degree required.
  • Excellent client and employer references.

Compensation:

  • Base salary + uncapped commissions.
  • Stock options package.
  • Health & dental insurance, 401(k) plan, and commuter benefits.

To apply, send your resume to jobs@offerpop.com.
