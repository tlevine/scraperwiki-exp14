Marketing Analyst
MD - Rockville
Administrative
Full-Time

We have an immediate opening for a Small Business Partner marketing analyst. This position will be primarily responsible for managing the identification of qualified business partners to support Westat’s contracting goals. Duties will include negotiating business partner arrangements, analyzing business metrics, and leading initiatives to improve business processes related to contract teaming. This position will also support the Westat marketing team with activities including opportunity identification and tracking. The successful candidate will be knowledgeable about Federal small business contracting regulations and requirements, and experienced in representing a prime contractor at small business recruiting events. Strong writing and communication skills, logical thinking, problem-solving and organizational skills, attention to detail, and the ability to work well in a team environment are essential. Proficiency with Microsoft Office applications, particularly Excel and Access, is required. A bachelor’s degree with 2 years’ experience supporting small business contracting activities and 5 years’ experience in the Federal services contracting market are required.

Any offer of employment will be contingent upon receipt of acceptable results from a background screening based on the specific position which will include, at a minimum, criminal records history.
